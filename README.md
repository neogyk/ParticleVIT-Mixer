# ParticleVIT-Mixer
HEP Event Tagging using GraphViT/Mixer

Particle tagging is the common task in many HEP studies. Effectiveness of the solution algorithm for tagging relies on the data representation, symmetries and interaction modelling of the event substitutes particle and the Neural Network architecture. We extend the list of the solutions with the Vision Transformer that use the graph representation of the particles as input.

Particle tagging is a crucial task in various High Energy Physics (HEP) studies. This work investigates the application of Vision Transformers (ViT) for particle tagging, leveraging a graph representation of particles as input. The effectiveness of particle tagging algorithms depends on factors such as data representation, modeling of particle interactions and symmetries, and the neural network architecture employed. By incorporating graph-based information into the ViT architecture, we aim to capture the relationships between particles and enhance tagging performance. This approach has the potential to improve upon existing techniques, particularly for tagging specific particle types. We present the details of our proposed method and discuss potential applications in HEP experiments.
